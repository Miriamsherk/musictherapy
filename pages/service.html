Individual Music Therapy Sessions

Individual music therapy sessions are provided at Ann Arbor Music Therapyâ€™s Studio located at 2890 Carpenter Rd Suite 1500 in Ann Arbor.

In-home music therapy sessions are available to clients unable to be seen at our studio space and will take place in your own home, assisted-living, treatment, or residential facility.


Group Music Therapy Sessions

Group sessions can be contracted directly by schools, therapy centers, treatment facilities, assisted-living facilities, or skilled nursing facilities.

Group music therapy sessions can also be formed by us based on age, developmental level, and common clinical focus. Group sizes range from 2-6 people.


Ongoing Social Skills Groups

Social Skills Music Therapy Group 10 week sessions are offered in the Fall, Winter, and Spring. These groups are for Elementary through High School aged children and teens focused on developing emotional and social skills through music with peers. Groups are made up of no more than 8 clients. Please email for details.


Contact Us

Ann Arbor Music Therapy

The mission of Ann Arbor Music Therapy, LLC is to provide exceptional music therapy services to assist clients of all ages and abilities in reaching their fullest potential through music. Ann Arbor Music Therapy, LLC provides person-centered, integrative, and strength-based approaches to address the needs of the whole person.
