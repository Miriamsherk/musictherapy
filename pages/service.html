In-home music therapy sessions will take place in your own home, assisted-living, treatment, or educational facility.



Group Music Therapy Sessions

30 minute session

60 minute session

Group music therapy sessions can be formed by us based on age, developmental level, and common clinical focus. Groups provide the opportunity for clients to integrate skills developed in individual music therapy in a setting that facilitates interaction with their peers. Group sizes range from 2-6 people.

Group sessions may also be contracted directly by schools, therapy centers, treatment facilities, assisted-living facilities, or skilled nursing facilities.



  Sliding scale may be available for individual and group sessions and group session rates may be negotiable for facilities. Insurance is not accepted, but invoices can be provided to submit to your insurance company.
Ann Arbor Music Therapy

The mission of Ann Arbor Music Therapy, LLC is to provide exceptional music therapy services to assist clients of all ages and abilities in reaching their fullest potential through music. Ann Arbor Music Therapy, LLC provides person-centered, integrative, and strength-based approaches to address the needs of the whole person.
